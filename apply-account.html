Create Your Account

Accounts are now made directly through Supabase. You no longer need to send an application—just sign up on the login page.

  1. Go to the Login page.
  2. Enter the email and password you want to use.
  3. Click Sign up. Supabase will create your account instantly.
  4. After signing up, you will be logged in and taken to the dashboard.
Your high scores and access level are stored in Supabase. If you ever request an upgrade, admins review it inside the site—no emails needed.
