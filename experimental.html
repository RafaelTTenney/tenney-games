How to add a new experiment

  1. Duplicate one of the <section class="card"> blocks above.
  2. Replace the title, description and the <pre class="demo"> content with your snippet or instructions.
  3. Link a separate HTML/JS file in the "Run" action if the demo is larger than a snippet.
